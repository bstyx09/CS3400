Mobile Interface Design


Web-enabled mobile devices are becoming increasingly prevalent, and understanding their strengths and limitations is the key to creating well designed interfaces with these devices in mind. W3.org specifies standards and best practices for these devices, which have unique constraints.

As W3.org points out, a number of the barriers that mobile users face are similar to those of users with disabilities. For example, the lack of a mouse or separate keyboard is often an overlapping concern.
